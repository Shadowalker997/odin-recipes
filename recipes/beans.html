Beans

Description

Beans is a special protein based food in Africa. It is regarded as the most healthy traditional food in Africa.

It is high in protein as well as fats and oil. Some people prepare it with vegetables to make it a more balanced diet

Ingredients

  • Raw white beans
  • palm oil
  • Salt
  • Maggi cube
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Boil the raw beans in water for about 45 minutes
  2. Fry onions in preferred quantity of palm oil
  3. Add the boiled beans
  4. Add some pepper
  5. Add maggi
  6. Add salt
  7. Add water and allow some time
