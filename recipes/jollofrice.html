Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is an African meal. It is mostly eaten during the day. Jollof rice is one of the meals mostly served at events in Africa beause it is acceptable around the world

The food was first made by a Nigerian chef though Ghanians like to take credit for it

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh Pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Tomato Paste
  • Butter for extra taste
  • Maggi cubes
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the meat
  2. Fry the tomato paste with onion
  3. Blend the tomatoes, pepper, onions
  4. Fry the blended tomatoes after the tomato paste
  5. Add the meat stock to the fried tomatoes
  6. Add maggi,salt,pepper
  7. Add your parboiled rice
  8. Add some water and allow to cook
